PLANET HOPPER

Launch between gravity fields, adjust paths using thrusters, and land safely inside the target wormhole.

FLIGHT CONTROLS

1. Orbit Navigation

Ship orbits planets automatically. Press A/D or ◀/▶ keys to change rotation angle.

2. Launching

Press SPACEBAR or click the screen to launch off the planet surface. Launching consumes 5% Fuel.

3. Trajectory Thrusters

In space, steer with W,A,S,D or Arrow Keys. Thrusting consumes fuel continuously. Path projection line shows gravity effects.

4. Docking Threshold

Land below 4.0 km/s to dock safely. Landing too fast destroys the vessel. Docking partially refuels your ship.
